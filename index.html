Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our painting and cleaning services.

How long does a typical painting project take?

The duration of a painting project depends on several factors including the size of the area, the complexity of the work, number of coats required, and current weather conditions. A typical interior room can take 1-3 days, while larger projects may take a week or more. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project. We pride ourselves on completing work efficiently without compromising quality, and we always communicate any changes to the schedule promptly.

What types of cleaning services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive cleaning services including residential cleaning, commercial office cleaning, post-construction cleanup, deep cleaning, and specialized surface cleaning. Our services cover everything from basic dusting and vacuuming to detailed carpet cleaning, window washing, and sanitization. We use eco-friendly cleaning solutions and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your space is not only clean but also safe and healthy. We can customize a cleaning package to meet your specific needs and budget.

Do you provide warranty on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. All painting projects include a 2-year warranty covering paint quality and workmanship. If you notice any issues within this period, we'll address them at no additional cost. We use premium quality paints and materials to ensure durability and longevity. Our warranty reflects our confidence in the quality of our services and our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Can you work around my schedule?

Absolutely! We understand that your time is precious, whether you're a homeowner or business owner. We offer flexible scheduling options including early morning, evening, and weekend appointments. For businesses, we can arrange work during off-hours to minimize disruption. We'll work with you to find a schedule that fits your needs. Contact us to discuss your availability, and we'll create a customized plan that works for everyone.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Getting a quote is easy and free! Simply contact us via phone, email, or our website with details about your project. We can provide an initial estimate based on your description, or we can schedule an in-person consultation where our team can assess your space and provide a detailed, personalized quote. Our quotes are transparent with no hidden fees. We'll discuss all options, materials, and pricing before you decide to move forward. Most quotes are provided within 24-48 hours.

About Sparkle & Shine

Sparkle & Shine was founded in 2014 by a team of passionate professionals who shared a common vision: to transform properties and create spaces that truly shine. What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into a trusted name in the painting and cleaning industry across Canada. Our journey has been built on the foundation of hard work, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Over the past decade, we've had the privilege of serving hundreds of satisfied customers, from residential homeowners to commercial businesses, each project strengthening our reputation for quality craftsmanship and exceptional service. Today, we continue to grow while maintaining the same values and personal touch that made us successful from day one.

At Sparkle & Shine, we believe that every property deserves to look and feel its best. Our mission is to provide superior painting and cleaning services that enhance the beauty, health, and value of our clients' spaces. We are committed to sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials and methods that protect both your property and the environment. Our core values—integrity, quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction—guide every decision we make and every project we undertake. We invest in our team through continuous training and development, ensuring that every member of our crew meets our exacting standards. When you choose Sparkle & Shine, you're choosing a partner dedicated to transforming your vision into reality with professionalism, expertise, and genuine care.
