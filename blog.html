How to Prepare Your Home for Professional Painting: A Complete Guide

Proper preparation is the foundation of a successful painting project. This comprehensive guide covers essential steps including clearing and decluttering your space, protecting floors and fixtures with drop cloths and painter's tape, and assessing wall surfaces for damage and imperfections. Learn how to clean thoroughly, fill holes, and repair damage before painting begins. Discover the importance of addressing moisture issues, selecting appropriate paint colors and finishes, and ensuring proper ventilation. With over 500 completed projects, Sparkle & Shine demonstrates that meticulous preparation ensures superior, long-lasting results that transform your home beautifully.
